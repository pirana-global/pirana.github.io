Careers at PIRANA

PIRANA is building a world-class technology ecosystem. Join us and shape the future of AI, automation, and intelligence.


1. Manager Internship Program (MBA Preferred)

Managers lead their respective teams and work directly under the Founder. They build & manage technical, business, marketing, and analytics units.

Eligibility: MBA, BBA, or strong leadership background.

Nature of internship: Unpaid

PPO Opportunity: Yes (Performance-Based)

Selected Managers will oversee hiring, training, task allocations, client operations, and project execution.


2. Technical Internship Roles

PIRANA recruits technical interns under each Manager.

  • AI & ML Intern
  • Data Science Intern
  • Automation Intern
  • Cloud Intern
  • Full Stack Developer Intern
  • Mobile App Developer Intern
  • Computer Vision Intern
  • RPA Intern

Nature of internship: Unpaid

Certificate Provided: Yes

PPO Opportunity: Yes (Performance-Based)


3. Business, Strategy & Operations Interns

These interns support the company backbone in parallel with technical teams.

  • Business Development Intern
  • Marketing Intern
  • Finance Intern
  • Research Intern
  • Operations Intern

Why Join PIRANA?

  • Work closely under the Founder
  • High-impact global projects
  • Get certified by PIRANA Global
  • Opportunity to lead teams
  • Performance-based PPO opportunities
  • Exposure to premium AI & automation products
